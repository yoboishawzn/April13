Ridgewood Towers

Ridgewood Towers is a premier residential destination in Taguig, just minutes from Bonifacio Global City (BGC). This modern high-rise offers a perfect blend of comfort, security, and accessibility for both short and long-term stays.

Each unit is thoughtfully designed with contemporary interiors, fully furnished living spaces, and floor-to-ceiling windows that provide abundant natural light. The building features resort-style amenities including a lap pool, children's pool, and a fully equipped fitness center.

Residents enjoy 24/7 security, high-speed internet, and dedicated property management. Whether for business or vacation, Ridgewood Towers offers a peaceful sanctuary in the bustling metro.

Fame Residences

Fame Residences stands tall in Mandaluyong City, offering contemporary urban living with unmatched convenience. Located along Sheridan Street, this development is known for its sleek architecture and vibrant community.

The property features spacious 1-bedroom units with smart layouts, premium finishes, and breathtaking city views. Residents enjoy direct access to the pool, gym, and function rooms.

With 24/7 security and high-speed internet ready units, it's perfect for young professionals and families exploring Metro Manila.
